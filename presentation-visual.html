Annual refresher for THQ staff — all spaces, all panels, troubleshooting procedures.

System orientation for every incoming officer or senior staff. No institutional knowledge lost on personnel changes — ever.
Software & Firmware
Q-SYS updates and firmware maintenance included. No surprise programming invoices for standard updates.

Leadership onboarding is specifically designed for THQ. Officer and staff transitions are frequent in the Salvation Army. Fathom Media ensures the AV system never feels foreign to anyone in the building, regardless of when they arrived or how long they've been in their role.
